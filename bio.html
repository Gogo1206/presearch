Microbiological, Biochemical & Biomedical Resources

Websites

PyMOL

PyMOL is the most popular protein/molecular viewer used by researchers around the world. In this software, you can visualize, build and create publication quality images for proteins.

AutoDock Vina

AutoDock Vina is one of the most popular and accessible molecular docking softwares, and can be adapted for drug discovery through virtual screening.

AlphaFold2

AlphaFold2 is the most popular and accurate software for protein structure prediction. It combines homology modeling with machine learning to predict a structure model for amino acid sequences.

OCHEM

OCHEM is a web server that can be utilized for computational biology and cheminformatics modeling, most notably of Quantitative Structure Activity Relationship modeling, which predicts the activities of biochemical compounds and molecules

BLAST

NCBI BLAST is utilized for nearly all structural or phylogenetic research, most notably for protein discovery, sequence analysis, alignment and modeling
